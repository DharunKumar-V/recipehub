Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 oz (225g) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (plus extra for serving)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced pancetta or bacon and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  3. Combine Ingredients:
    1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with a little salt and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Mix Everything:
    1. In the same skillet used for the pancetta, add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Remove from heat.
    2. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine with the garlic and pancetta drippings.
    3. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add some reserved pasta water if needed to reach your desired consistency.
    4. Stir in the crispy pancetta or bacon.
  5. Serve:

    Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
