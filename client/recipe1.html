Strawberry Custard

Hereâ€™s a simple recipe for strawberry custard.

Ingredients:

For the Custard:

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For the Strawberry Compote:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Make the Custard:
    1. In a medium saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it starts to steam, but don't let it boil.
    2. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch until smooth.
    3. Gradually whisk in a small amount of the hot milk into the egg mixture to temper the eggs.
    4. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining milk, whisking constantly.
    5. Continue to cook over medium heat, whisking constantly until the mixture thickens and starts to bubble gently.
    6. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
    7. Transfer the custard to a bowl and cover the surface with plastic wrap to prevent a skin from forming. Let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until chilled.
  2. Make the Strawberry Compote:
    1. In a medium saucepan, combine the strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice.
    2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the strawberries break down and the mixture thickens slightly, about 10-15 minutes.
    3. Let the compote cool to room temperature.
  3. Serve:

    Spoon the chilled custard into serving dishes and top with the strawberry compote. Enjoy your homemade strawberry custard!
